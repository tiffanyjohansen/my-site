About Me

The person
behind the work.

Tiffany J.

Designer, thinker,
detail obsessive.

Hi — I'm Tiffany, a visual and brand designer based wherever good coffee and natural light exist. I've spent the last five years helping companies, startups, and creatives build design systems and brand identities that feel honest and alive.

My work lives at the intersection of strategy and aesthetics. I don't believe in decorating things — I believe in designing them. Every choice should have a reason, and every reason should serve the person on the other side of the screen.

Before going independent I worked in-house at a couple of agencies in New York, which gave me a solid foundation in fast-paced creative environments and a deep respect for deadlines.
